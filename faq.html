Common Questions

What is SGPA?

SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) is the average grade point obtained in a single semester. It is calculated based on the grades earned in all subjects and the corresponding credits.

How is CGPA different from SGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the overall average of SGPA across multiple semesters. It reflects the student's academic performance throughout their course.

How do I calculate CGPA?

To calculate CGPA, you need to input your SGPA and credits for each semester. The calculator will then compute the average to give you the CGPA.

Can I use this calculator for any grading system?

The CGPA calculator works for most credit-based systems, but make sure you know how your institution's grading system works to input the correct values.
